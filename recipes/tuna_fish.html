Tuna Fish Salad

Description

A simple tuna salad to eat as a sandwich. It is quick and easy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans tuna
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 3/4 cup Sweet pickle relish

Steps

  1. Hard boil the eggs.
  2. Peel the eggs when done.
  3. Chop up eggs.
  4. Open cans of tuna, drain, and put in a mixing bowl.
  5. Mix mayonnaise into tuna.
  6. Mix relish into tuna and mayonnaise.
  7. Mix the eggs into the rest of the ingredients.
  8. Mix everything thoroughly.
  9. Enjoy.
