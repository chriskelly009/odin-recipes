Red Beans and Rice

Description

This is a simple but tasty recipe. It doesn't take long to make, and it leaves you full and satisified.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cans Red Kidney Beans
  • Instant Rice
  • 1 package of turkey sausage link
  • Onion Powder

Steps

  1. Cut up the turkey sausage into small pieces.
  2. Brown the turkey in a skillet.
  3. Add the kidney beans.
  4. Cook the rice according to directions on box.
  5. Season the beans and turkey with onion powder.
  6. When rice is done. Serve the beans and turkey over it.
  7. Enjoy.
